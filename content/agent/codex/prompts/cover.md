Create a wide cinematic cover image for a technical article about "Harness Engineering" — the practice of building production environments for AI coding agents.

## Visual Concept

Depict an isometric blueprint-style scene showing a sophisticated "workbench" system designed for AI agents. The central element is a luminous, semi-transparent AI agent silhouette (geometric, non-humanoid — think a glowing cube or polyhedron) sitting at the center of an engineered harness structure.

Around it, flat-vector style elements represent the five pillars of a harness:
1. Document pipelines (scrolls/blueprints flowing into the agent)
2. Boundary walls (clean geometric barriers with checkpoint gates)
3. Feedback loops (circular arrows connecting output back to input)
4. Testing scaffolding (grid-like structures with green checkmarks)
5. Observability monitors (screens showing logs and metrics)

The color palette is cool-toned: deep navy (#0A1628) background, engineering blue (#0F4C81) primary, cyan (#00B4D8) accents, with white (#FFFFFF) for text and highlights.

## Style Requirements

- Flat vector illustration style with clean geometric shapes
- Isometric perspective with slight depth
- 40-50% whitespace/breathing room
- No realistic humans — only abstract geometric agent shapes
- Clean lines, no textures or gradients (solid fills only)
- Title text "Harness 工程" in clean sans-serif, prominently placed in upper portion
- Mood: technical sophistication, calm confidence, engineering precision

## Composition

- Title "Harness 工程" positioned in upper-left area
- Central harness structure occupying 60% of the frame
- Cool navy background with subtle grid pattern suggesting blueprint paper
- No watermark
