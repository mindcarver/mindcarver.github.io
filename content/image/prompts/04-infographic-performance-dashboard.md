回测绩效评估仪表盘 — 四维指标体系

Minimalist hand-drawn line art infographic dashboard. 2×2 grid layout with four metric zones. Soft rounded cards, thin black strokes, muted pastel fills on clean white background. Doodled mini-icons in each zone header.

ZONES:
- Zone 1 (top-left) 收益指标:
  - 总收益率: 累计 P&L
  - 年化收益率: (1+R)^(252/N) - 1
  - Mini icon: upward arrow trending line
  - Fill: light mint (#B5E5CF)

- Zone 2 (top-right) 风险指标:
  - 年化波动率: σ × √252
  - 最大回撤: (cum - cummax) / cummax
  - Mini icon: warning triangle
  - Fill: light coral (#FFD5C2)

- Zone 3 (bottom-left) 风险调整收益:
  - Sharpe = (年化收益 - 无风险利率) / 年化波动率
  - Reference line: Sharpe > 1 为优秀
  - Mini icon: balance/scale
  - Fill: light blue (#A8D8EA)

- Zone 4 (bottom-right) 交易统计:
  - 胜率: P(日收益 > 0)
  - 偏度 / 峰度: 收益分布特征
  - 正收益均值 vs 负收益均值
  - Mini icon: bar chart
  - Fill: light lavender (#D5C6E0)

CENTER: Small title card "绩效评估仪表盘" with hand-drawn underline decoration

LABELS: "总收益率", "年化收益率", "年化波动率", "最大回撤", "Sharpe比率", "胜率", "偏度", "峰度", ">1 优秀"
STYLE: Hand-drawn line art, soft rounded rectangle cards in 2×2 grid, thin black strokes, muted pastel fills per zone. Doodled icons, small decorative dots. Clean white background. Card titles in slightly larger handwritten font. Values shown as placeholder metrics.
